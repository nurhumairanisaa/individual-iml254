    Hometown: Ipoh

    Ipoh, Perak, Malaysia

    Known for its beautiful limestone hills, Ipoh is where I spent my childhood. The city is rich in culture and history, offering both modern amenities and traditional charms. I love the food here, especially the famous Ipoh white coffee!

    Ipoh is also famous for its vibrant food scene, particularly its unique blend of Chinese and Malay cuisines. The food here is an integral part of the city’s charm, with traditional dishes passed down through generations. From street food to fine dining, there’s something for every palate.

    Best Restaurant Recommendation: Nifayyi Warung & Kafe

    If you're ever in Ipoh, I highly recommend visiting Nifayyi Warung & Kafe, one of the most famous restaurants in the city. Known for its signature Salted Egg Chicken Dish, it’s a must-try dish that captures the essence of local flavors. The restaurant is always bustling with locals and tourists alike, and their tender chicken paired with butter rice is simply unbeatable. Don’t forget to try the Chicken Chop as well, which are crispy and delicious!
